Frequently Asked Questions:

The bride and groom to be have compiled this helpful list of frequently asked questions for the convenience of both you and the couple.

When is the wedding?

The wedding is on June 20th of 2020 in the town of Duck in Outer Banks, North Carolina.


Where should I stay?

We have blocked hotel rooms at the Hampton Inn Corolla and the Baymont by Wyndham. If you are planning to stay for the whole week, the Outer Banks also offers a number of vacation properties for rent. Keep in mind the Outer Banks is a big island so make sure you look for accommodations near the towns of Corolla, Duck, and Southern Shores.


How do I get there?

The Outer Banks is accessible from Norfolk International Airport (82 miles ~1.5 hours north) or Raleigh Durham International Airport (192 miles ~4 hours west).

For those who would prefer to travel by car, The Outer Banks is a scenic 7-9 hour drive from New Jersey.
