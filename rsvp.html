RSVP:

RSVPs are handled through The Knot's RSVP system. Each response will generate an email for Tim and Victoria. Here are some tips to make sure your RSVP experience is as smooth as possible.


The search engine on this site is not the best (not my fault), so try searching using your full name or any variations of your name.


RSVPs are grouped by invitation so if your household received more than one invitation, there will be more than one reservation. Make sure all of the names match those of your family members to ensure you have fond the correct reservation. You must RSVP for your whole group.


Please let us know where you are staying by dropping us a note.


If you are planning to come to either of the other two celebrations, please let us know in the notes section as well.


If you have issues with the website or are afraid of technology, feel free to reach out to us over email or send your RSVP by mail.
